I am an assistant professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Ohio State University. My research develops wireless network and communication systems that are high performance, reliable and secure. My interests include networking protocols, measurements, communication systems and security. I received my Ph.D from Stanford University in Electrical Engineering in 2010. Subsequently, I joined UT Austin as a post doctoral researcher. I joined Ohio State in Fall 2011.
